Services

What we do

Our repair shops can handle any repair, on any bike (no matter where you bought it!), from a quick flat tire fix to an in-depth overhaul of every system on your bike. We're proficient with modern technology plus the classic stuff, so we're equipped for what you ride and any repair it may need.

Every REI shop offers the following service packages—in addition to a complete range of repairs—to keep your trusty ride in prime condition, or to bring a long-neglected bike back to life:

Bronze Tune:
If your bike is in good shape, our entry level package includes everything necessary to keep it that way.

It includes:

  • Wheel truing
  • Brake and shifting adjustments
  • Drivetrain lubrication
  • Tire inflation
  • Full safety inspection

Silver Tune:
Our most popular service is the perfect choice for a bike that sees regular use, or even one coming out of storage.

It includes:

  • All services in the Bronze Tune
  • Precision bearing adjustments to the steering system and wheels
  • Light cleaning of the bike's frame and wheels
Gold Tune:
If your bike sees heavy use, especially on pavement, take advantage of this package.

It includes:

  • All services in the Silver Tune
  • Deep cleaning of the entire drivetrain in our ultrasonic cleaners, to remove stubborn road grime
  • Thorough re-tensioning of spokes to ensure long life of your wheels

Platinum Tune:
For the ultimate in performance, this service ensures a long-lasting reliable ride.

It includes:

  • All services in the Gold Tune
  • Thorough disassembly, cleaning and re-greasing of all bearing systems for long life
  • Our most thorough wheel true service ensuring the smoothest ride possible
